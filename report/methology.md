# Market Signal Collection Methology

## Overview
The goal of this is to define what a relevant market signal for Revolut looks like. This ensures that all collected signals are consistent and relevant.
Collected signals are **public news or events** rather than speculations about Revoluts internal strategy.

## Market Signal Definition
- External (Public)
- Recent (Not older than 12 months)
- Facts, not opinions

## Signal Types
### Product and Feature launches
New Product launches or platform feature launches.

### Partnerships
Newly announced partnerships.

### Expansion
Announcements about expanding into new countries or markets.

### Hiring
Postings of looking for new workers and new leadership announcements.

### Financial Reports
Revenue growth/drop, fundraising events.

## Data Sources
Signals will be collected from:
- Official Revolut press releases
- Fintech news outlets
- Punlic company statements

## Exclusions
- Rumours
- Opinion-based articles

## Output
This step is a manual collection of data that will be stored in 'data/revolut_signals.csv'
