# Definition of Go-To-Market(GTM) Categories

## Overview
The goal is to define the categories of GTM and then applying them to the collected signals.

## Categories
### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
Signals that target specific users or industries.

### Timing and Expansion
Signals about new expansions, launches that affect when/where a GTM event can happen.

### Messaging
Signals about partnerships, product launches or security features that influence customer messaging.

### Financial
Financial milestones, investments that can effect the credibility of the GTM.

### Organizational
Leadership hiring, approvals that influence how ready the GTM is.

## Output
